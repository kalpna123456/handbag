A BRAND STORY OF STYLE AND SUBSATNCE


In a country where fashion trends often come at a premium, Bag X emerged as a beacon of hope for those seeking stylish handbags without breaking the bank.
Founded on the simple yet powerful mission of providing Pakistanis with high-quality bags that are both affordable and fashionable, Bag X has quickly become
a beloved brand among the young, trendsetting, and savvy shoppers. Understanding the struggle of wanting to express personal style without sacrificing one's bu-
dget, Bag X set out to create a brand that resonated with the desires of the modern Pakistani woman. By combining quality craftsmanship with innovative designs,
Bag X has successfully bridged the gap between affordability and luxury.
